About Me

Born and raised in Chicago, IL, I am the oldest of four siblings. I studied Pyschology in Undergrad, but always had a passion for creative design growing up. My interest in web design sparked when I took a Graphic Design course in High School. When I learned that coding skills with a focus in Front End Development would allow me to use design to construct webpages, I dove right in. I am someone who loves new challenges and learning new things. My goal is to contribute to projects that feed my passions while also playing a role in the needs of business development.
